CHAPTER 33: 
Football match

Yekaterinburg arena is the largest sports facility in the city. Recently a new reconstruction has been carried out in order to bring the stadium in compliance with FIFA requirements for 2018 World Cup Russia including installation of 35 000 seats. After the championship, temporary seats will be disassembled and the capacity of the stadium will go down to 25 000 spectators.
