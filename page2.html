Visual diary of unfilmed neverending road-movie

Two sisters

In my childhood I lived with my older sister and mother. My sister were constantly fighting with my mother, bringing her to tears, and threatening her. As a child she behaved very cruelly. Meanwhile I was always near to calm my mother down. Later my sister left us, as I thought, to live with my father, and I safely forgot about her. I was about 12 years old, when my mother told me about my childhood, she mentioned that I was a terrible child, I used to drive my grandmother, father and mother to tears. There has been no sister. I haven’t told anybody the way I remembered it. I'm scared for myself.

Picnic

I went to a picnic with friends and got into a car accident. But everything went out well: we got out without a scratch. I drove back home, spent 2 weeks just doing the usual things: going to work, meeting with friends. I felt so well and so peaceful, and then ... I woke up. It turned out that all this time I had been in a coma, I was the only one who had survived. But it felt as if I was living through these two weeks...

The pill

When I was 19 years old, my friends offered me to try some strange pills. In answer to my question: "what is this?", they replied: "this is fun, sister." I took the pill, it had no effect on me. I went home, did household chores and went to bed. Suddenly I felt something pouring on me. It was rain. I opened my eyes.. and I realized that I was lying on the ground in a courtyard, all beaten up, with a broken tooth and with no clothes on except for a skirt, tights and someone's bra. I don’t remember what happened.
